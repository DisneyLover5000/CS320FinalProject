Precedure:

1. For this project you need to gather all of your materials.

2. next open all of the rings ( using your pliers pick up the ring in both pliers and twist only moving one plier).(see picture 1)

3. grab a ring of the 1st color and close it repeat with the same color.

4. next grab a ring of the next color put the new ring throught the 2 closed rings and close the new ring.

5. repeat with the same color.(see picture 1)

6. grab a ring in the next color and loop it threw the two ring of color 2, then close.repeat with second ring of the same color.(see picture 2)

7. the 2 rings of color 3 fold over rings of color 2. (see picture 3) and then grab a ring of color 1 and thred it through the rings of color 3 inside color 2(see picture 4).

8. repeat steps 4-7(see picture 5) until you have the length you want. (tipicaly they are about 22 sections long (see picture 6 for section size)
